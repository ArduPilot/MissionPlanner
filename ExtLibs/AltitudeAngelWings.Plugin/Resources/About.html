Altitude Angel Mission Planner Plugin

This Mission Planner plugin enables use of Mission Planner with Altitude Angel's GuardianUTM Cloud platform. The GuardianUTM Cloud platform provides worldwide and trusted airspace and ground hazard data directly on the Mission Planner maps, making it easier to discover where you can and cannot fly, and plan for safer flights.

If you have any feedback, problems or questions about this plugin, please log a support ticket with Altitude Angel, or raise a GitHub issue with Mission Planner.

For more information, visit our website where you can also find our privacy policy and terms and conditions.
